Alaska Flight Status

Description: Alaska Airlines creates unique flight status pages for every flight. They are created 4 days in advance and live for 2-3 days after the flight. Enter the flight number and choose Today, Tomorrow, etc and then Check Status to open that specific flight status page..

Note: Alaska updates flight status pages approximately 6 minutes after the top of each hour.

This tool is unofficial and not affiliated with Alaska Airlines. Always confirm flight details at alaskaair.com.
